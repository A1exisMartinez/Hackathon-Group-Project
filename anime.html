ANIME

Anime can stimulate imagination and creativity in yourself. Most anime stories are based on a world that is different from the real world in varied ways, some have supernatural characters or powers, some revolve around a sense of wonder, others just daily high school life. These curated animated worlds can provide both a place of famliarity and escape from the real world. Find your next one here!

SPORTS

One of the best way to relax and get mental health support is by finding something that will keep you active and your body in shape. By working out or having a sport to keep you in shape, can be one way to to help your mental health.
